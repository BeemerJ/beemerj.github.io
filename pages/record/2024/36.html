Artist: Bob Sinclar

Album: Paradise

Year: 1998

Genre: French House

Review

The quintessential French Touch record...


What a fun vibey album all around. Every single track is filled with crazy samples and instantly danceable 909 goodness.It's about as french house as they come, even has a cameo from Daft Punks one and only Thomas Bangalter

Aside from the drama behind Gym Tonic the album has some absolute bangers I keep on coming back to and just admiring the sampling on display with funky guitars and some of the grooviest bass lines to ever come out of the scene.


I highly reccomend this album to fans of anything funky. Move your body...
