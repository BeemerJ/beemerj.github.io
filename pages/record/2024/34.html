Artist: Source Direct

Album: Exorcise The Demons

Year: 1999

Genre: Drum and Bass

Review

This Album FUCKS...


If you're ever looking for a downright dark and oppressive soundscape with weird squelches and filthy sounding breaks, this is the album for you.

The first track I heard was "Call and Response" and on first listen I immediately had to check out the rest of it. Tight drumming, trance like to a point even, and a really dirty mix throughout.


Without a doubt one of the grungiest DnB records I've ever heard.
